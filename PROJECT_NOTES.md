## Project Context

This is a separate PWA app and website for the rural town of Martindale, Texas 78655.

The site was created to give the town a modern, easier-to-maintain communication hub. The old website is outdated, hard to edit, and locked behind paid maintenance from past vendor decisions.

The main goal is to improve communication, access to information, and town modernization while keeping the experience simple enough for older and rural community members to use comfortably.

The project was originally created about three months before active maintenance resumed. The mayor of Martindale will relay town information and requests as needed.

The highest-value outcome is reducing the town's communication and notification gap.
